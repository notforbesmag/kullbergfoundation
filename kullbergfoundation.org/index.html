The Kullberg Foundation encourages Fellows to pursue their dreams and goals of fighting for a social cause, whether that is in the immediate future or part of their long-term plans. The Kullberg Scholarship believes that personal dreams and ambitions are shaped and influenced by previous experiences and people, providing a foundation on which to launch a successful future.

ABOUT THE KULLBERG FOUNDATION

The Kullberg Scholarship is named after Teresita and Lennart Kullberg, who through education and ambition, have utilized their unique skillset to persevere in the path of changing the world, one life at a time.

PARTNERING WITH EMZINGO

Building communities and making a social impact are at the core of the Kullberg Foundation's principles. Emzingo's award-winning NexGen Fellowship is an excellent way to promote these principles. Through the Emzingo Fellowship, the Scholars are able to obtain hands-on experience in a developing country using their unique skillset to have a real impact on the community.
